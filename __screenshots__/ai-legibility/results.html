About These Checks

These checks measure how easily AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) can discover, read, and understand your content. Each tier represents a dependency layer:

Critical — Can AI agents reach and read my content?

Important — Can AI agents find all my content and understand what it's about?

Optimization — Does my content present well when cited?

Critical — Gates AI Discovery

  • robots.txt — Controls whether AI crawlers are allowed on your site
  • sitemap.xml — The canonical sitemap format most crawlers use
  • Homepage content — Homepage returns real content, not an empty SPA shell

Important — Improves Discovery Quality

  • llms.txt — Direct signal to LLMs about what content to index
  • sitemap.txt — Plain-text supplement to XML sitemaps
  • Sample pages — Pages in your sitemap return real content
  • Meta tags — Title and description for summaries

Optimization — Enhances Presentation

  • JSON-LD — Structured data for entity understanding
  • Open Graph tags — Social preview and citation formatting
  • Canonical URLs — Prevents duplicate content confusion
